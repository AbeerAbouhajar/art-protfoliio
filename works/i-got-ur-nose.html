Oil on Canvas2024

I Got Ur Nose

This painting is based on an archival photograph of my cousin and her nephew, capturing a playful and affectionate moment. Within the archive of Deep Almasri, there is a recurring presence of such lighthearted interactions, revealing a dimension of everyday joy that runs alongside more formal or constructed narratives.

This work focuses on that sense of spontaneity, where gesture and expression take precedence over precise detail. While rooted in a specific image, the painting reflects on how memory preserves emotion rather than exact form.

The playful exchange becomes symbolic of connection, intimacy, and continuity within the family. Through this, the work highlights how archives are not only sites of loss or distance, but also spaces where warmth, humour, and lived experience remain active and felt.
