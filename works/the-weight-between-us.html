The Weight Between Us

This work draws from a photograph of Deep Almasri and his wife during their time in Saudi Arabia, a period marked by displacement and transition. The drawing explores the subtle tension within the image, where closeness and distance coexist.

Influenced by Svetlana Boym's notion of reflective nostalgia, the piece dwells in the past's emotional complexity. The charcoal marks emphasise what sits between the figures — an unspoken weight shaped by time, memory, and the conditions surrounding the moment.

The space between them becomes as significant as their presence.
