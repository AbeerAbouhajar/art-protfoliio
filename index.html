About Hanin

Born in Homs, Almasri moved to Ireland in 2014, where her practice has since developed across different stages of life. Her artistic journey has evolved alongside education, beginning with the Leaving Certificate and continuing into third-level study.

Currently completing a degree in Fine Art and Design Painting at Limerick School of Art and Design. Her work reflects an ongoing engagement with material exploration and personal development, shaped by both lived experience and formal training.

Through painting, her visual language continues to be refined, balancing technical development with an intuitive approach to making, evoking themes of identity, displacement, exile, and connection. The human figure remains central to her practice, acting as a consistent anchor through these explorations.
